Work

Selected highlights, organized by impact. This is not a resume.

Infrastructure Re-platform

The existing OpenStack infrastructure was underperforming and expensive. Customers were hitting resource ceilings, and cost of goods was climbing.

I led a full rebuild of the OpenStack platform, redesigning compute, storage, and networking layers from the ground up.

Result: 2-3x performance improvement, 30% reduction in COGS, and 2x customer density on the same hardware.

Project Leviathan

A legacy UCaaS platform running on on-premises VMware needed to move to the cloud. The migration had to be seamless for customers and support modern orchestration.

I architected and led the migration from on-premises VMware to Kubernetes on AWS, building the deployment pipelines and infrastructure automation from scratch.

Result: fully cloud-native platform on Kubernetes with automated deployment, improved reliability, and the operational flexibility to scale on demand.

AI Agent Orchestration

Engineering teams were spending too much time on repetitive implementation work that followed well-defined patterns.

I built an AI agent orchestration platform that takes product specifications and produces production-ready pull requests, complete with ephemeral preview environments for review.

Result: significant reduction in time from specification to production code, with built-in quality gates and automated testing.

Open-Source Code Review

The team was paying $12,000 per year for a commercial code review tool that did not meet our needs.

I built a replacement in 8 hours using AI-assisted development, then open-sourced it.

Result: better tool, zero cost, and a public contribution back to the community.

Security and Compliance Programs

Multiple organizations needed SOC2 and HIPAA compliance but had no existing programs, policies, or technical controls in place.

I designed and implemented compliance programs from scratch: policies, technical controls, audit preparation, and ongoing governance.

Result: successful SOC2 and HIPAA certifications across multiple organizations, with programs that continue to pass audits.

Unity Platform

MSP customers needed a single platform to manage their business: marketplace, CRM, access control, and identity.

I led the engineering team that built Unity, an integrated platform with marketplace, CRM, role-based access control, and single sign-on for MSP customers.

Result: a unified platform that consolidated multiple tools into one system, reducing operational complexity for MSP partners.

Mentorship

Talented people were stuck in non-technical roles with no clear path into the industry and no resources for expensive bootcamps or degrees.

I mentored individuals through hands-on learning, pairing real projects with structured guidance.

Result: people who started on factory floors now hold six-figure technology careers, with zero education cost to them.
